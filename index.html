Privacy Policy for QuickV Notes

Last updated: June 2026

Information We Collect

QuickV Notes does not collect, store, or transmit your personal notes to any external server.

Data Storage

QuickV Notes stores notes locally on your device. The application does not upload your notes to our servers. Data stored on your device is subject to the security protections provided by your device's operating system. Users should keep their devices secured with appropriate screen locks and security settings. QuickV Notes is not intended for storing highly sensitive information such as passwords, banking credentials, government identification numbers, or medical records currently.

Microphone Permission

The microphone permission is used only to convert speech into text when you choose to use voice input.

Notifications

Notification permission is used only to display reminders created by you.

Data Sharing

QuickV Notes does not collect or transmit your personal notes or settings to any external server. All notes are stored locally on your device. The app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect certain device information, such as advertising identifiers, IP address, and diagnostic information, to provide and improve advertising services.

Data Deletion

You can delete notes from the app at any time. Uninstalling the app removes locally stored data, subject to your device backup and restore settings.
